# AIGP Exam — Short Guide & Practice Resources

**Quick overview**

The **AIGP exam** is a professional certification that evaluates candidates on applied concepts, governance, and practical skills needed to plan, implement, and maintain AI systems responsibly. This short article gives a concise study approach, recommended resources, and SEO-friendly meta information you can use on GitHub or a blog.

---

## Why this certification matters

AI projects must balance innovation with accountability. Passing the AIGP shows employers you understand both the technical and policy sides of AI deployment — from risk assessment and validation to monitoring and responsible use.

## Rapid study plan (2–4 weeks)

1. **Foundations (3–5 days)** — Review core concepts: model lifecycle, data governance, fairness, explainability, and compliance.
2. **Hands-on practice (7–14 days)** — Work through practice questions, small labs, and scenario-based tasks that simulate real-world governance challenges.
3. **Review & exam strategy (3–7 days)** — Consolidate weak areas with targeted quizzes and timed mock exams.

## What to practice

* Interpreting model evaluation metrics in context.
* Designing data governance and labeling pipelines.
* Incident response for model drift and bias discovery.
* Writing short governance checklists and risk mitigation plans.
